Automation Starter: Build Your First AI Flow

A beginner-friendly, soul-aligned mini course designed to help small business owners and creators build and launch their first AI-powered chatbot flow.

Course Overview

This mini course teaches small business owners and creators how to build their first AI-powered chatbot flow, combining conscious design principles, automation strategy, and ready-to-use templates. The course is structured in 3 modules covering automation clarity, building a chatbot flow, and launch strategies. Each lesson includes video content, downloadable resources, and clear action steps to help participants create a functional AI assistant aligned with their brand voice and business needs.

By the end of this course, you will have built and launched your first AI flow that truly represents your business and serves your customers in an authentic way.

Module 1: Automation Clarity – What to Build First

This module helps participants gain clarity on what type of automation to build first, ensuring they choose the most valuable option for their specific business needs.

Lesson 1.1: Welcome to Automation Starter

Objective: Orient learners to the course journey and set intentions.

Content:

  • Brief course welcome video (2–3 min)
  • Course promise: You will launch your first AI flow by the end of this course
  • Walkthrough of the course structure (3 modules, downloads, templates, community)
  • Mindset for soulful automation: this isn't about replacing humans—it's about reclaiming your time
  • Reflective journal prompt: "Where in your business do you feel stuck or overwhelmed?"

Action Steps:

  • Watch welcome video
  • Download and review course roadmap PDF
  • Complete reflection journal prompt

Lesson 1.2: Why Soul-Aligned Automation?

Objective: Understand the philosophy behind using AI and automation with integrity.

Content:

  • Video lesson (5–6 min): What "soul-aligned automation" means
  • Why automation isn't just about efficiency — it's about freeing you to do your most creative, impactful work
  • Examples of bad vs. good automation (robotic vs. personalized experiences)
  • Importance of tone, voice, and mission in every automated message

Action Steps:

  • Watch lesson video
  • Fill out the "Automation with Integrity" values checklist (PDF)
  • Optional: Post in the community one business process you'd LOVE to offload

Lesson 1.3: Map Your First Automation

Objective: Choose one business process to automate.

Content:

  • Video walkthrough (8–10 min) of the 3 essential starter flows:
    1. Booking Assistant (automates calendar scheduling + intake)
    2. Lead Qualifier (captures + filters new leads with logic-based questions)
    3. FAQ & Support Bot (answers top questions 24/7)
  • How to map a simple workflow with arrows and steps using the provided Flow Map PDF
  • Decide which one is right for your business right now

Action Steps:

  • Download the Flow Selector PDF
  • Complete the flow-mapping exercise (fill in trigger, message, response, and output)
  • Choose your ONE flow to build in this course

Module 2: Build Your First Chatbot Flow

In this module, students learn how to define their AI assistant's personality, set up the technical aspects, and implement a functional chatbot for their chosen use case.

Lesson 2.1: Meet Your AI Assistant

Objective: Define your chatbot's voice, purpose, and boundaries.

Content:

  • Video lesson (6 min): What is an AI assistant and why should it reflect you?
  • Define your assistant's tone (inspired by your brand)
  • Write your assistant's purpose statement (1-sentence function)
  • Persona building: boundaries (what it can and can't do)
  • Use the Persona Builder Worksheet

Action Steps:

  • Download and complete the AI Assistant Persona Worksheet
  • Write your chatbot's purpose + tone
  • Optional: Post your chatbot name + vibe in the group for feedback

Lesson 2.2: Setting Up Your Chatbot

Objective: Use templates to build a functional chatbot.

Content:

  • Video walkthrough (10 min): Set up your bot using a drag-and-drop interface or AI assistant (like GPT)
  • Duplicate provided templates:
    • Booking Assistant Prompt Template
    • FAQ Bot Prompt Template
    • Lead Qualifier Template
  • Replace placeholder text with:
    • Your business name
    • Scheduling link (Calendly, Acuity, etc.)
    • Contact preferences (email, phone)
  • Preview what the conversation flow looks like

Action Steps:

  • Choose and customize your chatbot template
  • Use the Starter Prompt Template to test your own input/output
  • Save your chatbot flow

Lesson 2.3: Embed and Share Your Chatbot

Objective: Launch your chatbot for real-world interaction.

Content:

  • Video (5 min): Where and how to use your chatbot
    • Embed on website
    • Share via link in email or Skool
    • Add to a landing page or PDF guide
  • Importance of context (who's clicking the link?)
  • How to test before going live

Action Steps:

  • Generate chatbot link
  • Embed it in one place (site, page, linktree, etc.)
  • Share with 2 friends or colleagues for first impressions

Module 3: Launch + Learn

The final module focuses on testing, refining, and successfully launching the chatbot, with strategies for announcing it and collecting feedback for improvement.

Lesson 3.1: Test + Refine

Objective: Run test scenarios and fix common issues.

Content:

  • Video: Common beginner mistakes
  • Roleplay: How to test your chatbot by pretending to be your customer
  • What to check: clarity, tone, logic, broken links
  • How to tweak your prompt or response instructions

Action Steps:

  • Run 3 test conversations
  • Update any language that feels stiff or unclear
  • Use the Checklist to finalize edits

Lesson 3.2: Launch Your Flow

Objective: Announce your chatbot to your audience.

Content:

  • Sample email + social post templates
  • How to introduce AI without scaring people
  • Ways to make it fun (name your bot, include GIFs or rewards)

Action Steps:

  • Choose 1-2 platforms to announce (email, Skool, IG, etc.)
  • Use the Launch Checklist
  • Post your bot in the community with a "Meet My Assistant!" thread
